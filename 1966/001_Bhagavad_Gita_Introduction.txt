- Bhagavad Gita 
* is also known as Gitopanishad.
* is essence of Vedic knowledge
* is important of various Upanishads.

- Why another English commentation of Bhagavad Gita?
* None of the many editions are strictly authoritative.
* As everyone has expressed their own opinion through
the commentation of the Bhagavad-gita, without touching the
spirit of Bhagavad-gita as it is.
* The Bhagavad-gita should be taken or accepted as it is
directed by the speaker Himself, similar to - as medicine is
taken as directed by the physician and label on the bottle.

- Person accepted Sri Krishna as the Supreme Personality of
Godhead (Bhagwan)
* All acharyas.
* Sankaracharya, Ramanujacharya, Madhvacharya, Nimbarka Swami
and Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu.
* In Brahma-samhita, and all Puranas.
* Bhagavata Puranam : SB 1.3.28 
Krishnas tu bhagavan svayam.

- We should take Shrimad Bhagavad-Gita as it is directed by the 
Personality of Godhead.
* BG 4.1-3
imaṁ vivasvate yogaṁ

manur ikṣvākave 'bravīt

sa kāleneha mahatā
yogo naṣṭaḥ parantapa

- 3 classes of transcendentalists
* Gyani - impersonalist
* Yogis - meditator
* Bhakt - devotees

- Shrimad Bhagavad Gita is understood by a person
* Who has similar qualities like Krsna.
* He must be a devotee.
* He must be in relation, direct relationship with the Lord.
* As soon as one becomes a devotee of the Lord, he has a direct
relationship also with the Lord.
* BG 10.14 - You (Krsna) cannot be known by even the demigods, who 
are greater personalities than the human being. And how a human 
being can understand Sri Krsna without becoming His devotee?
* Therefore Shrimad Bhagavad Gita should be take up in spirit of 
devotee of Lord Sri Krsna.
* One should not think that he is equal, on the same level of 
Sri Krsna.
* One should not think Sri Krsna to be an ordinary personality, or
maybe a very great personality.
* Should understand Sri Krsna to be the Supreme Personality of
Godhead, with submissive spirit.
* Receive Shrimad Bhagavad Gita in a submissive spirit and oral
reception, otherwise it is very difficult to understand.

- 5 ways of devotee in relationship with Krsna.
* Devotee in a passive state.
* Devotee in active state.
* Devotee as a friend.
* Devotee as parent.
* Devotee as conjugal lover.
* Everyone has got a particular relationship with the Lord, and is 
evoked by the perfection of devotional service. And is forgotten
in present status of our life.

- Arjuna accepted Shrimad Bhagavad Gita as :

arjuna uvāca
paraṁ brahma paraṁ dhāma

puruṣaṁ śāśvataṁ divyam
ādi-devam ajaṁ vibhum

āhus tvām ṛṣayaḥ sarve
devarṣir nāradas tathā
asito devalo vyāsaḥ
svayaṁ caiva bravīṣi me

sarvam etad ṛtaṁ manye
yan māṁ vadasi keśava
na hi te bhagavan vyaktiṁ
vidur devā na dānavāḥ.

- Purpose of Shrimad Bhagavad Gita
* Is meant for delivering persons from the nescience of this 
material existence.
* Everyone of us is always full of anxieties due to our this
material existence.

- A perfect human being
* One is awakened to this position that "Why I am suffering? 
I do not want all these sufferings. I have tried to make a 
solution of all these sufferings, but I have failed."
* Humanity begins when this sort of inquiries are awakened
in one's mind.
* In Brahma-sutra this enquiry is called brahma-jigyasa.
Athato brahma jigyasa.
* What I am, why I am suffering, wherefrom I have come or 
where I shall go after death.
> He is practically right student for understanding 
Shrimad Bhagavad Gita.
> Also he must have respect (shraddhavan), a fond respect in 
the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

- 5 different truths comprehended by Shrimad Bhagavad Gita
* What is God?
> There is a Supreme controller.
* Constitutional position of jiva (living entities).
* Prakriti.
* Time.
* Karma.
